First off I wanted to say thank you to all of you for agreeing to be a bridesmaid!  We are super excited to have you all be a part of our wedding.

I wanted to share thoughts on bridesmaid dresses. I don't like the idea of having matching bridesmaid dresses. I want everyone to pick a dress that makes them comfortable and shows their unique style. Below I included some images with the general look I am going for as well as thoughts on what to look for in a dress.

Inspiration
Dress Requirements
  1. Blue - Any of shade works
  2. Long - Thinking floor length or close. Lots of website filters call this maxi length.
  3. Unique - I would love for everyone to have a different dress. When you find your dress, share it with us.
Dress Considerations
  • Warmth - We are planning to have a short outdoor ceremony (weather permitting). Since the dresses will be long it should be easy to wear tights/leggings/thermals and warm socks underneath. I am thinking about getting matching blanket scarves or sweaters for everyone to provide some extra warmth on top.
  • Material - There are tons of different options: satin, chiffon, lace, velvet, etc. Feel free to choose whatever you like best.
  • Pattern/Design - Most dresses I found when I was searching were solid colors. If you find a dress you like with sparkles, flowers, stripes, polka dots or any other details go for it. I think it will add a fun unique vibe so don't feel like you have to avoid these types of things.
  • Cut/Fit - Anything works here so pick something that you like and you feel good in.
  • Price - Again this is whatever you are comfortable with. Don't read too much into the prices on the example dresses I have listed out below.
Potential Dress Options

You don't have to pick any of these dresses, they are just some options we found that could be a good fit or give you an idea of what to look for. Some of them have limited sizes currently or may be sold out by the time you take a look. Another thing to note is that a lot of the dresses out right now are mostly for summer and winter dresses will likely come out later in the year. Many stores have free returns which makes it easy to order a few options and find the one that you like best. Additionally, if you don't want to buy a dress you could look into renting one from places like rent the runway.
