Hey Everyone,

First off I wanted to say thank you to all of you for agreeing to be a bridesmaid!  We are super excited to have you guys be a part of our wedding.

I wanted to share my thoughts on bridesmaid dresses. I don't like the idea of having matching bridesmaid dresses. I want everyone to pick a dress that makes them comfortable and shows their unique style. Below I included some images with the general look I am going for as well as thoughts on what to look for in a dress.

Inspiration
Dress Requirements
  1. Burgundy, Blush or Champagne - Ideally we have a mixture of color between everyone.
  2. Long - Floor length.
  3. Unique - I would love for everyone to have a different dress. I think a good mix might be to have 1-2 dresses with a print to add texture + interest. Additionally it would be cool to have some sparkle somewhere in there.

When you find a dress you like, share it with us.

Dress Considerations
  • Warmth - We are planning to have a short outdoor ceremony (weather permitting). Since the dresses will be long it should be easy to wear tights/leggings/thermals underneath. I am thinking about getting matching blanket scarves or sweaters for everyone to provide some extra warmth on top.
  • Material - There are tons of different options: satin, chiffon, lace, velvet, etc. Choose whatever you like best.
  • Pattern/Design - If you find a dress you like with sparkles, flowers, stripes, dots or any other details go for it. I think it will add a fun unique vibe, so don't feel like you have to avoid these types of things.
  • Cut/Fit - Anything works here, so pick something that you like and feel good in.
  • Price - Again this is whatever you are comfortable with.
Potential Dress Options

You don't have to pick any of these dresses, they are just some options we found that could be a good fit or give you an idea of what to look for. Some of them have limited sizes currently or may be sold out by the time you take a look. Another thing to note is that a lot of the dresses out right now are mostly for summer and winter dresses will likely come out later in the year. Many stores have free returns which makes it easy to order a few options and find the one that you like best. Additionally, if you don't want to buy a dress you could look into renting one from places like rent the runway.
